Mercedes-Benz Dealer Network

SEM • GTM • Reporting

The Challenge: A multi-location Mercedes-Benz dealer network needed sophisticated SEM account structure, go-to-market strategy, and cross-channel reporting to coordinate campaigns across locations.

Our Approach: We designed and implemented a scalable SEM account structure that enables both centralized oversight and location-level optimization. We developed a comprehensive go-to-market framework and created unified reporting dashboards for visibility across all channels and locations.
